Biscoff Cookie Pie
Moustache Milk and Cookie Bar offers a range of different things, but their cookie pies are just something else. Though I love eating
desserts, I am quite senstive to desserts that are too sweet. The Biscoff Cookie Pie, in my opinion is still a bit too sweet but
delicious nonetheless. I like to enjoy it by putting it in the microwave for 10 seconds for the ultimate soft, gooey and chewy
experience.
