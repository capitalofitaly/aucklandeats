The OG
This is absolutely, by-far, one of my favourite dumpling places here in Auckland. I try to go here as often as I can, when I'm
just craving for some good ol' dumplings. You definitely are gonna wanna have to try this place out. Their dumplings are not
your typical dumplings, as their wrapping is a little breadier, but that's what makes it so good. The pairing with the sauce and
filling brings you into food heaven! 10/10 would reccomend.
